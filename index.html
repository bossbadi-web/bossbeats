BossBeats

A smarter music bot with support for Spotify, SoundCloud, Twitch, stream URLs, file uploads, and more!

YouTube support has unfortunately been removed due to violations of YouTube's Terms of Service. The bot will now use SoundCloud as the default music provider. I made a clone of this bot with YouTube support if that's your thing, but it will remain unverified for obvious reasons. If you want first dibs, be sure to invite the new bot to your servers before it hits the limit. Thank you all for using BossBeats!
Command Description
join Join your voice channel
play Play a song from keywords, url, or file upload
search Search for a song or get the previous search results
queue Show/add song to the music queue
seek Skip to a specific time in the song
volume Show/change the volume (0 to 1000)
pitch Show/change the player's pitch (0.1 to 5.0)
speed Show/change the player's speed (0.1 to 2.0)
skip Skip to the next song in the queue
nowplaying Show the currently playing song
loop Toggle the player's loop
shuffle Randomize the order of songs in the queue
pop Remove a song from the queue
move Reposition a queued song
pause Pause audio
resume Resume audio
stop Stop audio
clear Clear the queue
leave Leave your voice channel and clear the queue
radio View the available radio stations/playlists
reverse Reverse the order of songs in the queue
renode Reconnect to the current music node (debug)
